Classic Sponge Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat eggs and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add vanilla extract and mix well.
  5. Gently fold dry ingredients into the egg mixture.
  6. Heat milk and melted butter until warm, then fold into the batter.
  7. Pour batter into the prepared cake pan.
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  9. Let cool before serving.

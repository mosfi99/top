Why mechanical?

Mechanical keyboards offer superior durability, tactile feedback, and customization compared to regular keyboards. They are built with individual mechanical switches for each key, providing a more satisfying typing experience. Additionally, they are highly customizable, allowing users to choose keycaps, switches, and layouts that suit their preferences. Whether for gaming, programming, or general use, mechanical keyboards enhance productivity and comfort.
